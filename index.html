SNU FINTECH AI 12th

Seoul National University's K-Digital Training program — a 7-month intensive to train industry-ready experts in Big Data, AI, and Fintech. Supported by the Ministry of Employment and Labor (KDT).

Apply / Contact
Full scholarship via 국민내일배움카드
Start: Summer 2026 · Limited seats
Program at a glance
  • Duration: 7 months (Modules 1-3: 4 months, Module 4: 3 months project)
  • Format: Full-time · in-person (weekdays)
  • Target: Recent grads (within 2 years) and career-changers
  • Contact: snufin99@gmail.com

Curriculum

Core modules covering AI foundations, fintech, and hands-on practice.

Module 1–3

Foundations & Applied AI

AI math, statistics, Python, ML/DL, NLP, databases, visualization, fintech product design.

Module 3

Advanced Topics

Algorithmic trading, blockchain, crypto & fintech law, digital currency, insurtech use cases.

Module 4

Capstone Project

3-month full-time project with partner companies — real datasets, deliverables, and presentations.

Capstone & Industry Partners

Partner companies propose real problems and host student teams for immersive projects.

How it works

Companies submit projects → teams are matched → full-time 3-month execution → mid/ final presentations → shared ownership guidance.

Benefits

Intern experience, networking, potential recruitment, and practical outcomes such as prototypes or pilot platforms.

Past partners

AIA, SK Energy, Yes24, IBK, Samsung Fire, KPMG, and more.

Faculty & Mentors

Top professors, industry leaders, and practitioner mentors.

Professor Ryoo Geun-Kwan

Economics & Data Science — Stanford PhD; lead faculty providing macroeconomic and statistical depth.

Industry Practitioners

Entrepreneurs, lawyers, and fintech engineers delivering guest lectures and workshops.

Mentoring

Teams work closely with SNU faculty and PhD-level teaching assistants during projects.

Apply / Contact

Applications and inquiries via email. Funding is available through the 국민내일배움카드 (no tuition burden for eligible candidates).

Who can apply

Graduates or soon-to-be graduates within 2 years who want to specialize in BigData & Fintech or shift careers.

How to apply
  1. Email a short CV and motivation to snufin99@gmail.com
  2. Screening & interview with program staff
  3. Enrollment and orientation
